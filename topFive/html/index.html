TOP FIVE'S PICKS FOR APRIL 2019

With the help of our community filled with foodies from all around. Each month we curate a list of the five hottest resturants located through out the north Dallas area and provide you with the one stop shop for your next mouth watering adventure.

OMG TACOS

Ever out late with your friends and were craving some good authentic tacos?? Well the search is over. OMG Tacos supplies the variety that will fill your cravings with their different options of burritos, tacos, nachos, and many more delicous items. With 5 locations spread across the DFW area open everyday from 11AM to 3AM there's never an excuse to not stop by for a quick bite to eat

RAMEN HAKATA

With 3 locations open so far. Their broth is prepped 36 hours before serving and you have a choice between miso ramen and Tonkotsu which is known to be one of Japan's top three styles of ramen. Want genuine Japanese ramen? Ramen Hakata is the place to go.

SHELL SHACK

One of a kind seafood restaurant experience specializing in proprietary infused seasoned crab and seafood of all shapes and sizes.

NORTH ITALIA

Every day there is a new delicious favorite made from scratch, like the Chef’s daily pizzas, waiting to be discovered and devoured. A tempting assortment of wines, beer, and house-made cocktails give you endless ways to treat yourself. From the moment you walk through their doors, to your last scoops of budino at the end of a satisfying meal, you’ll know you’re in good hands.

LEGACY FOOD HALL

Legacy Hall brings new meaning to the dining and drinking experience to Plano, Texas. Equal parts premium food hall, live entertainment destination, beer garden, and craft brewery. LFH is a community committed to bringing the best that local chefs and entertainers have to offer in one unique space for an unforgettable experience.
